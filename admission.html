Checklist

Required Documents

Birth Certificate

Original Birth Certificate issued by Corporation/Municipality/Panchayat along with a photocopy.

Aadhaar Card

Copies of child's Aadhaar card. If child doesn't have one, parent's Aadhaar copy is required.

Passport Photos

Recent passport-size photographs of the student (4 copies) and parents (2 copies each).

Transfer Certificate (TC)

Original TC from the previous school (mandatory for Class 2 onwards) and counter-signed if required.

Address Proof

Ration Card / Voter ID / Passport / Latest Electricity or Landline Telephone Bill copy.

Community Certificate

Photocopy of the Community Certificate issued by the competent authority (if applicable).

Step-by-Step

Admission Process

1

Enquiry & Registration

Visit our school office to collect the admission form or fill out the online enquiry form. Registration is the first step towards securing a seat.

2

Document Submission

Submit the completed application form along with all the necessary documents mentioned in the checklist at the school office.

3

Interactive Session

Students and parents will be invited for a friendly interactive session with the school authorities. This helps us understand the child's readiness.

4

Admission Confirmation

Upon selection, complete the fee formalities to confirm admission. We welcome you to the Bushra School community!
